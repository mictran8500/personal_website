Projects

Genetic Algorithms for Solving Sudoku Puzzles

The use of genetic algorithms have proven successful for a number of unique applications. Domains that utilize GAs range from game theory equilibrium resolution, to artificial creativity. GAs have also found success in solving numerous NP-complete problems, such as the Traveling Salesman Problem. While all NP-complete problems are equally difficult in regards to general computational complexity, some problems offer a much more advantageous representation for GAs. Thus, allowing GAs to be more effective in solving that specific set of problems. Our goal was to determine if Sudoku falls within that set.

Lets Eat

Some friends and I built an application that can be used when you can't decide on a place to grab food from. Imagine Yelp, but besides of giving an entire catalog of places, it randomly chooses a place based on your input criteria. The motivation to build this app is simple enough, because humans are indecisive. I'm sure every single one of us has been in the situation where we have can't make a decision on where to eat. Whether it be from indecisiveness or an absence of agreement from the group, we've all been there. Our intended purpose for the app is that whenever someone uses it, they stick with the chosen destination and that's that.
